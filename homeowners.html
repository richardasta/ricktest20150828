CONSTRUCTION DUST GETS EVERYWHERE.

Remodeling projects can generate literally pounds of dust particles. Tiny pieces of cement, silicates, lead, wood, insulation and dirt from demolition; sawing lumber; cutting tile, countertops, and flooring; sanding drywall and more. Dust that goes up in the air – getting on you, your children, your pets, furniture, clothes, towels, toothbrushes. In your lungs. Dust that gets everywhere. And stays around even months after the remodeling project is over.

BUILDCLEAN HELPS KEEP YOU AND
YOUR FAMILY SAFE.

The BuildClean Dust Control System eliminates up to 90% of airborne construction dust, making your home more livable during construction. It’s a revolutionary new tool that your remodeler can use to provide continuous and highly effective air filtration during remodeling, virtually eliminating airborne dust. Quiet and easy-to-use, the pre-filter captures large dust particles, while the HEPA-certified second-stage filter traps microscopic particulates in the air as small as one micron in diameter. Keeping you and your family safe.

Plastic dust barriers, shop vacs and fans just don’t do enough to control dust. And brooms and vacuuming just stir it up. When your contractor uses BuildClean, airborne dust is actually trapped and contained within the job work area – and eliminated. So you don’t have dust migrating to areas outside the construction site. Or find construction dust lingering in your home months after a remodel. BuildClean lets your contractor manage remodeling dust so you don’t have to.

HELPING MAKE YOUR REMODEL MORE LIVABLE.

As more of us choose to stay in our homes during a remodel, managing dust and maintaining good indoor air quality during and after remodeling is becoming even more important. BuildClean – the foundation of a complete dust control plan – is the dust control system that the best remodelers are choosing to make homes more livable. So before your next home remodeling project, talk with your contractor about dust control and ask him to use the BuildClean Dust Control System - the industry standard in livable remodeling.
